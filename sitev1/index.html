NW3 Weather is an observation site located near Hampstead, in North London, UK.
The site was established with an automatic, server-linked personal weather station in July 2010 and runs continuously.
The Home page is updated every minute; the rest of the live data pages and graphs are updated every 5 or 10 minutes. More info can be found on the About page.
